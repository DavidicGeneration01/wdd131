Abuja is the capital and eighth most populous city of Nigeria. Situated in the middle of the country within the Feferal Capital Territory (FCT), it is a planned city built mainly in the 1980s based on a master plan by International Planning Associates (IPA). Abuja Georgraphy is defined by Aso Rock (a 400-metre monolith). In addition, there are other interesting areas such as Zuma Rock (a 792-metre monolith), Presidential Complex, National Assembly, Supreme Court, etc.
