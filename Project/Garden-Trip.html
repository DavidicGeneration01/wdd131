Pick-Your-Own Berries & Veggies

Experience the joy of harvesting your own food! Wander through our designated PYO fields and fill your basket with the season's best. Perfect for families and food lovers.

Details:

  • Season: Strawberries (June-July), Tomatoes (Aug-Sept), Pumpkins (Oct) - *Check current availability!*
  • Duration: As long as you like during PYO hours!
  • Pricing: Pay by the pound/container. See current rates on arrival.
  • Suitability: All ages, family-friendly.
  • What's Included: Guidance on picking areas. Containers available for purchase or bring your own (check policy).

Related Products: Find pre-picked berries, homemade jams, and fresh pies at our Farm Stand!

Guided Farm & Garden Tours

Join one of our knowledgeable guides for an insightful walk around the farm. Learn about our history, sustainable growing methods, crop rotation, and what's currently thriving in the garden.

Details:

  • Season: Spring through Fall (Check schedule).
  • Duration: Approx. 60 minutes.
  • Pricing: $15 per adult, $8 per child (under 5 free). Booking recommended.
  • Suitability: Curious minds, ages 6+.
  • What's Included: Guided walk, Q&A session.

Related Products: Grab a farm map or guide book from our shop.

Hands-On Workshops

Deepen your connection with nature and learn new skills! We offer a variety of seasonal workshops, from beginner gardening and composting to jam making and flower arranging.

Details:

  • Season: Offered year-round (indoors/outdoors depending on topic). Check schedule for specific dates.

What to Bring/Wear

  • Comfortable walking shoes (fields can be uneven).
  • Weather-appropriate clothing (layers recommended).
  • Sun hat, sunscreen, sunglasses.
  • Water bottle.
  • Reusable bags/baskets for PYO (or purchase here).
  • Camera to capture memories!

Farm Rules & Tips

  • Please stay on marked paths.
  • Supervise children closely, especially near equipment.
  • Only pick in designated PYO areas.
  • No pets allowed in PYO fields or Cafe (service animals welcome).
  • Handle plants and produce gently.
  • Pack out what you pack in.

Accessibility

Main paths, Farm Stand, and Cafe are wheelchair accessible. Some PYO areas may have uneven terrain. Accessible restrooms available. Please call ahead with specific needs.
